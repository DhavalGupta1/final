108 Ambulance Service

Government Emergency Medical Service - Free ambulance with trained paramedics. Call 108 for immediate medical transport.

Fire Tender

Municipal Fire Brigade vehicles. Call 101 for fire emergencies. Equipped for fire suppression, rescue, and hazmat situations.

Police PCR Van

Police Control Room patrol vehicles. Call 100 or 112 (universal emergency). For traffic control, law enforcement, and crowd management.

NDRF Teams

National Disaster Response Force - Specialized teams for floods, earthquakes, building collapse, and large-scale disasters.

Medical Teams

Specialized medical personnel from Government Hospitals. Includes doctors, nurses, and emergency medicine specialists for mass casualty incidents and critical care.
